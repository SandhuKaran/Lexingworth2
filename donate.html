How Your Donation Helps

$50

Covers the expenses for up to two students to join one of our professional development or networking events.

$150

Allows one student to receive a full day of technical finance training through one of our external training providers.

$350

Covers the expenses of one student for the entire year in our Investment Fund or Analyst Academy, ensuring they can receive technical training and attend all events without barriers.

$500

Funds a semester-long mentorship program for a student, pairing them with an industry expert.

$1000 +

Will more broadly contribute to the expenses related to larger-scale annual events. These events connect students with professionals and alumni as well as learn from distinguished speakers or compete in competitions for prizes.
